July 24–26, 2017

International Scientific Conference «Environmental Safety of Plant Protection»

The Conference is devoted to the 105-th anniversary of the Corresponding Member of academician of NAS of Belarus A.L. Ambrosova and 80-th anniversary of academician of NAS of Belarus V.F. Samersova. During the Conference there will be an opportunity to discuss problems of mutual interest in the field of plant protection what will help to establish new relations and initiate a cooperation with the scientists of other countries. Materials of the Conference prepared by the leading scientists of Belarus and near and far abroad countries, State plant protection and quarantine service workers, experts of practical agriculture will be of interest for scientific workers and practical workers of agriculture connected or interested in the problems of plant protection and prospects of its development in the XXI century. Conference materials will be printed till the moment of carrying out the Conference.
